How to drive with 3T in Norway

Do you want to become a driver with us? Are you interested in being unique in your work and establishing your own business? We give you the opportunity 3T offers you an excellent working environment and service 24 hours a day

Get Started

As usual, to start any business, you need some steps that you need to implement before starting your work, so we will try to help you find the right solution and get you to the last step and start working as soon as possible.

New to the taxi industry (I have no licenses today)

To be able to use the Uber app as an independent operator, you must have the following:

  • Valid taxi licence, or tour cart licence. Note: a trolley license only gives you access to the UberXXL product (16 passenger seats). Find more information by clicking here
  • Valid driver's license
  • A vehicle that meets the requirements for a taxi licence, company car license or touring car licence.

Experienced operator (I already have all licences/permits)

Are you an experienced operator who has a valid driver's license and a taxi licence? Sign up now and start driving in the next 1-2 days.

  • Valid taxi licence, or tour cart licence. Note: a trolley license only gives you access to the UberXXL product (16 passenger seats). Find more information by clicking here
  • A vehicle that meets the requirements for a taxi licence, company car license or touring car licence.

Run under a Fleet Partner (don't want to start my own business)

Don't you want to get your own vehicle or taxi licence? Then you can start driving for one of the Fleet Partners who are active on the Uber platform. Get more information below.

  • Valid taxi licence, or tour cart licence. Note: a trolley license only gives you access to the UberXXL product (16 passenger seats). Find more information by clicking here
  • Valid driver's license
